Questions & Answers

It depends on your pet's breed, coat type, and activity level. Generally, dogs with longer coats will require more frequent grooming than dogs with short coats. We recommend bringing your pet in for grooming every 4-6 weeks to keep their coat healthy and shiny.
Unfortunately, we do not allow pet owners to stay with their pets during the grooming process. This is for the safety of both the pet and the groomer, as well as to minimize distractions and ensure that the grooming process is as efficient as possible.
If your pet has special grooming needs, please let us know when you make your appointment. We will do our best to accommodate your pet's needs and ensure that they receive the appropriate grooming services.
We offer a wide range of grooming services for dogs and cats, including baths, haircuts, nail trimming, ear cleaning, and more. Please see our website or give us a call for a full list of our services and pricing.
The length of a grooming appointment depends on the type of service and the size and breed of your pet. On average, a grooming appointment can take anywhere from 1-3 hours. We will provide you with an estimated time frame when you make your appointment.
